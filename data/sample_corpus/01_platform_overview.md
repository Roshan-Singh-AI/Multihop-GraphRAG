# Helios Platform Overview

Helios Platform is the central analytics layer for the manufacturing intelligence program. Helios Platform receives curated event streams from Mercury Data Lake and surfaces dashboards for production, quality, and maintenance teams.

Orion Gateway connects plant-floor devices to Helios Platform. The EdgeOps Team owns Orion Gateway rollout, patching, and configuration management.

WeldVision Camera streams weld inspection frames through Orion Gateway. WeldVision Camera is used by Atlas Plant and Nova Plant for weld quality checks.

Mercury Data Lake feeds Helios Platform with normalized telemetry, inspection results, and asset events.
