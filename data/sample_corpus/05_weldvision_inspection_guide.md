# WeldVision Inspection Guide

WeldVision Camera is the computer-vision inspection component used for automated weld quality checks. WeldVision Camera routes image frames through Orion Gateway before archival in Mercury Data Lake.

WeldVision Camera is governed by QA-17 Standard when used for release-critical inspection. Atlas Plant and Nova Plant both use WeldVision Camera in release-critical inspection workflows.

Supplier Triton Robotics provides the WeldVision Camera hardware assembly.
