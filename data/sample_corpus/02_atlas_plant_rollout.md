# Atlas Plant Rollout Notes

Atlas Plant deployed Orion Gateway in Line 4 to support WeldVision Camera and Helios Platform reporting. Atlas Plant uses WeldVision Camera for inline weld quality checks on battery trays.

The Quality Systems Team reviews Helios Platform dashboards for Atlas Plant every shift. Atlas Plant is covered by QA-17 Standard for automated visual inspection traceability.

If WeldVision Camera inspection is paused at Atlas Plant, the Quality Systems Team coordinates with Compliance Desk before production resumes.
