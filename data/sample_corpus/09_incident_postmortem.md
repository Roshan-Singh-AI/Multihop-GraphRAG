# Incident Postmortem: Missing Weld Frames

An investigation found that Orion Gateway firmware 4.2.1 dropped a subset of WeldVision Camera frames during peak bursts. The missing frames affected Atlas Plant first and were later reproduced at Nova Plant.

The Incident Bridge connected EdgeOps Team, Quality Systems Team, and Compliance Desk. Compliance Desk required a documented workaround because QA-17 Standard covers WeldVision Camera evidence completeness.
