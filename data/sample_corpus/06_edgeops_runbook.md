# EdgeOps Runbook

The EdgeOps Team manages Orion Gateway lifecycle operations, including patching, rollback, and monitoring. The EdgeOps Team coordinates with Compliance Desk when Orion Gateway incidents can reduce inspection completeness.

When Orion Gateway degrades WeldVision Camera traffic, EdgeOps Team notifies Quality Systems Team and starts the Incident Bridge process. Incident Bridge routes escalations to Compliance Desk for plants covered by QA-17 Standard.
